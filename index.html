WHY AGRI·365

Built different_

🧠
Benthinck knows your farm
Benthinck reads your live machine data, soil scores, weather and crop stage before generating your morning task list. Not a generic chatbot — contextual intelligence per field and per machine.

Real machine connectivity
Live OAuth2 connections to John Deere, AGCO Fuse, CLAAS, Grimme, Horsch, CNH AFS, Kubota and SDF via MQTT and REST APIs. Not manual imports — actual telemetry, slip data, tyre pressure and ISOXML task sync.
🛸
Drone to task map in 24h
DJI inspection drone detects NDVI anomaly → anomaly triggers spray mission → DJI Agras T50 executes spot treatment → ISOXML task record closes the loop. 4,167× sharper resolution than Sentinel-2.
🌍
NL/EU and AU/US in one
One codebase, two unit systems. Switch between ha/bar/°C/€ and ac/PSI/°F/$ with a single toggle. Every value — tyre pressure, soil depth, yield, fuel cost — converts automatically throughout the entire platform.
🌾
Field-level P&L
Yield maps, treatment costs, machine hours and soil health scores combined into a true profit picture per field — not a farm-wide average. Know exactly which fields earn and which cost you money.
📉
Machine NPV & replace decision
A dedicated NPV waterfall per machine: book value, residual value, maintenance cost trend and OEE trajectory combined into one clear replace-or-repair verdict. Stop guessing — start calculating.
🌱
Soil science, not just pH
ECa resistivity, redox potential, bulk density, hydraulic conductivity, NDVI zones and groundwater levels — all per field, all linked to rotation advice and automatically generated variable-rate task maps for ISOBUS.
🎯
Scenario Simulation — decision assistant
Compare Current vs Optimal vs Drought vs Early Season side by side — yield, revenue and NPV per scenario. Benthinck scores each option and recommends the best path. Not a report to read — a decision to make.
🤖
Autonomous farm planning
Benthinck continuously reads weather forecasts, machine telemetry, soil health scores and crop stages to autonomously generate, prioritise and schedule your farm operations — without you needing to ask.
👥
People & task planning
A large farm runs on more than one person. Assign tasks to staff by day and week — with weather windows, sun position and moon phase as the leading priority framework. Overview by week, month and season.
🧬
Generational farm memory
Every Benthinck recommendation can be liked, overridden or commented on. Your reasoning is stored permanently — building a farm knowledge base that spans generations. The AI reads this history before every new recommendation, making advice progressively smarter for your specific farm.
📱
PWA — offline in the field
Installable on any phone or tablet. Works without internet in the field — machine checklists, field notes, spray logs. Data syncs automatically when back online. No app store, no update delays.
